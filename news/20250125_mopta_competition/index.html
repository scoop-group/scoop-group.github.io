2025-01-14 Advisory offer for AIMMS-MOPTA Optimization Modeling Competition

The 17th AIMMS-MOPTA Optimization Modeling Competition is an international competition open to graduate and undergraduate students. You can compete in teams of up to three participants for the prizes awarded for solving an aviation personell scheduling problem.
